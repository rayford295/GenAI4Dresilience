# 🌍 GenAI4Dresilience  
**Generative AI for Disaster Resilience**

---

## 📌 Overview

**GenAI4Dresilience** aims to explore how **Generative Artificial Intelligence (GenAI)** can reshape the full lifecycle of **disaster resilience**, including risk identification, monitoring, damage assessment, emergency response, and long-term recovery.

With the increasing frequency and intensity of disasters under climate change, traditional approaches face limitations in:

- Multimodal data fusion (RSI, SVI, social media, sensors)  
- Cross-scale understanding (local ↔ regional ↔ global)  
- Real-time decision-making under uncertainty  
- Interpretability and trust in AI-driven systems  

This project positions **Generative AI as a unifying paradigm** to address these challenges through:

- Cross-view generation (e.g., satellite → street view)  
- Semantic reasoning and knowledge synthesis  
- Agent-based autonomous decision-making  
- Human-AI collaborative disaster intelligence

The proposed framework conceptualizes how **Generative AI integrates into disaster resilience tasks** across the disaster lifecycle:

### Key Characteristics:

- **Multimodal Integration**: Remote sensing, street-view, social sensing  
- **Generative Capability**: Filling observational gaps and simulating scenarios  
- **Agentic Intelligence**: Autonomous decision-making via AI agents  
- **Explainability**: Toward trustworthy and fiduciary GeoAI

## 🔬 Research Scope

### 1. Risk Identification and Mitigation
- Generative simulation of hazard scenarios  
- Synthetic data generation for rare/extreme events  
- Spatial risk pattern discovery  

### 2. Disaster Monitoring and Early Warning
- Real-time multimodal data fusion  
- AI-generated alerts and predictive signals  
- Temporal dynamics modeling  

### 3. Damage Assessment
- Cross-view generation (Satellite → Street)  
- Multimodal damage perception (image + text)  
- Fine-grained severity classification  

### 4. Emergency Response and Decision Support
- AI agents for resource allocation  
- Scenario-based decision simulation  
- Human-AI collaborative planning  

### 5. Recovery and Long-Term Resilience
- Urban digital twins  
- Generative urban reconstruction  
- Policy and adaptation strategy support
